ABOUT US

We are dedicated to making government fiscal data accessible and understandable for everyone. Transparency is crucial for democratic engagement, but the complexity of financial information often acts as a barrier. Our mission is to simplify this data, empowering citizens, policymakers, and financial institutions to make informed decisions and engage in meaningful discussions about government finances. Our data comes from the Fiscal Data Treasury API, which includes key datasets like the Monthly Statement of Public Debt (MSPD) and the Monthly Treasury Statement. Our web application offers a user-friendly interface to access U.S. government financial data. Key features include:

Simplified Access: Easy navigation to comprehensive fiscal data.
Visualizations: Charts and infographics that present spending, revenue, and debt in a clear, digestible format.
Search and Filter: Tools to search and filter data by specific categories and periods.

We also serve a variety of stakeholders which include:

General Public: Enhancing their ability to participate in democratic processes with better information.
Policymakers: Supporting data-driven decision-making and effective public communication.
Financial Institutions: Providing clear insights into government spending to aid in financial forecasting and investment decisions.
