ABOUT US

We are dedicated to making government fiscal data accessible and understandable for everyone. Transparency is crucial for democratic engagement, but the complexity of financial information often acts as a barrier. Our mission is to simplify this data, empowering citizens, policymakers, and financial institutions to make informed decisions and engage in meaningful discussions about government finances. Our data comes from the Fiscal Data Treasury API, which includes key datasets like the Monthly Statement of Public Debt (MSPD) and the Monthly Treasury Statement.
